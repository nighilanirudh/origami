A lot of time and thought has gone into our campus to create a space students would love to come to every single day

Flexible Learning Spaces

We modelled our classrooms similar to ones found in Finland. Our classrooms are flexibly designed to suit the needs of any kind of session be it a lecture, a test, a group activity or an individual study session aiding the learning process

We don't restrict learning to classrooms, students are allowed to pull a chairs together in any free space on campus and study or colloborate during individual or group sessions.

All our classrooms are equipped with

Interactive Panels

In-built computer opens the possibilies to presentation of class material and interactive learning

Videos and 3D applications enable visual learning in students

The panels also serve as board ensuring a chalk and dust free environment

Noise reducing Windows

Our school is located just off one of the prime roads of Coimbatore, the Avinashi Road which also welcomes its noise.

To keep the noise at bay all the windows in our school are aqoustic windows reducing the disturbance caused by unnecessary sound

The role of classroom temperature in enabling better learning has been heavily underrated.

Science reports significance learning improvement when the room temperature is maintained between 23 degree centigrade to 25.

Hence, all our classrooms are air conditioned to keep the room temperature within this bracket ensuring that the tropical weather is no hinderance to learning.
