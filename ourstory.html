Our History

P N P Education Trust

The P N P Educational Trust was founded in the year 1987 with an aspiration to provide quality education and all round development to students. This was exactly what we achieved via our sister chain of schools, National Model.

National Model Schools

Over the past 3 decades, National Model has grown leaps and bounds to establish itself as the best group of schools in Coimbatore.

We can boast of student body currently 4610 students strong following the Matriculation & CBSE curriculum.

Our alumna are spread across the globe and do us proud with their success stories.
